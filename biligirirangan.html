General Information

This is the general information about the place...
Biligirirangan, also known as B.R. Hills, is a picturesque hill range located in the Chamarajanagar district of Karnataka, India. It is a captivating destination that encompasses a diverse ecosystem, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers. The hills are nestled between the Western Ghats and the Eastern Ghats, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Biligirirangan is home to the Biligiri Rangaswamy Temple, dedicated to Lord Ranganatha, which attracts devotees from far and wide. The region boasts a rich biodiversity, with dense forests, grasslands, and numerous streams that sustain a variety of flora and fauna. It is renowned for its wildlife sanctuary, a protected area where one can spot rare and endangered species such as elephants, tigers, leopards, gaur (Indian bison), and several species of deer and birds. Trekking and hiking trails traverse through the hills, offering an opportunity for adventure seekers to explore the region's natural beauty up close. The tranquil atmosphere, coupled with the awe-inspiring scenery, makes Biligirirangan a captivating destination for those seeking solace in nature's embrace.

Geological Information

This is the geological information about the place...
Biligirirangan Hills, also known as BR Hills, is a mesmerizing geological formation located in the southern region of Karnataka, India. This picturesque hill range is nestled amidst the Eastern Ghats and Western Ghats, offering a unique blend of both ecosystems. Geologically, BR Hills are predominantly composed of granite and gneiss rocks, which have undergone millions of years of weathering and erosion, resulting in their present form. The hills feature undulating terrain, interspersed with deep valleys, ravines, and numerous streams. The region is characterized by its rich biodiversity and is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including several endemic species. The geological history of Biligirirangan Hills, coupled with its diverse natural heritage, makes it a fascinating destination for nature enthusiasts, researchers, and those seeking to explore the geological wonders of the region.
